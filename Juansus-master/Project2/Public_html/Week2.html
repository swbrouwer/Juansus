Our Basic Branding Ideas

  • Buy.Vote.2015 is a service for those looking to exert their wealth to create a more appealing outcome in their country's elections. In addition it helps those disenfranchised with the political system get something from their vote other than empty promises and frustration

  • Buy.Vote.2015 is the middle man taking the risk for those wanting to illegally buy/sell votes in an election. The company must be slick and professional with little judgement to inspire confidence in its customers

  • Buy.Vote.2015 is the only platform enabling a vote economy and as the only option will remain on top of the market share by incorporating killer UX and the best security ever seen in an online platform
